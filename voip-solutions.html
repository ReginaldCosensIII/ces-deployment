VoIP Solutions

Since the early 2000s, CES has been at the forefront of VoIP technology enabling us to offer advanced, scalable VoIP services that improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Your Secure Network To Quality IT Service

CES offers a complete range of IP telecom solutions from sleek desk phones, receptionist consoles, Wi-Fi handsets, conference phones, and softphones. Enjoy seamless professional-grade communication for your team.

T-Series Phones

T-Series phones deliver HD voice, vibrant color displays, built-in Bluetooth, and dual-band Wi-Fi. Select models seamlessly integrate with Microsoft Teams, making them ideal for today's collaboration-focused, team-driven, modern workplaces.

Receptionist Console

A powerful and intuitive call-handling solution designed to enhance the productivity of front desk staff and administrative assistants. Boost productivity with 60 programmable keys for quick access to frequently used contacts, and features.

Wi-Fi IP Phones

Wi-Fi IP phones provide secure, high-performance wireless communication with enterprise-grade roaming and ergonomic, durable designs, enabling mobile professionals to stay connected across dynamic workspaces.

Conference Phones

Conference phones deliver professional audio for hybrid and in-room meetings, with 360° voice pickup, echo cancellation, and touch control. Native integration with platforms like Microsoft Teams ensures seamless scheduling and collaboration.

Softphone Solutions

When a traditional handset is not an option, CES offers softphone solutions that turn your computer or mobile device into a fully functional business phone. Perfect for remote work, you'll never miss an office call and are just an extension away. Plus, all outbound calls display your company number—maintaining a professional image.
